About Us

No matter what you’re looking for in a gym, we’ve got a membership option made for you. All Acne Fitness members enjoy unlimited access to their home club and the support of our friendly, knowledgeable staff anytime you need it. Extreme Subscription® members receive additional benefits, including the ability to have up to 5 people on 1 account and access to any of our workout videos, as well as live videos!

Looking for a little extra guidance or inspiration to help you meet your fitness goals? As a Extreme member, you can take advantage of our free fitness training or find your motivation by reading real member experiences on Planet of Triumphs. It’s our goal to provide a clean, safe, welcoming environment for anyone who really wants to work on getting more active.
